About Us

We are a a leading provider of emergency medical transportation in Kenya, offering a reliable and efficient ambulance booking web app. Our mission is to ensure timely access to life-saving care for individuals in need, connecting them with trained medical professionals and state-of-the-art vehicles equipped with essential equipment. With a focus on speed, safety, and professionalism, we strive to make a positive impact on the health and well-being of our communities.

Our Services

Emergency Ambulance Booking

Our web app allows users to quickly and easily request emergency ambulance services with just a few clicks, ensuring prompt response to medical emergencies.

Medical Escort

We provide specialized transportation services for patients requiring medical supervision during non-emergency transfers, such as hospital discharges or medical appointments.

Inter-Hospital Transfers

Our experienced team facilitates seamless transfers between medical facilities, ensuring continuity of care for patients in critical condition.
